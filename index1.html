About Assassin's Creed

Assassin's Creed is a series of action adventure games developed by Ubisoft Montreal and released in 2007 for the PlayStation 3, PC and PSP.

High quality

The extended view feature gives you a wider view of the environment, and dynamic lighting and sunlight effects allow you to immerse yourself in the dunes as far as your eye can see.

Open World

From lush forests to volcanic islands to bustling cities, explore a world shaped by gods and men and torn apart by a war. Show off your warrior abilities in a massive 100-man battle between Athens and Sparta, or charge through an entire fleet in a naval battle in the Aegean Sea.

Fluent Action

Your decisions will affect how this adventure unfolds. You can play multiple endings thanks to the new dialogue system and the decisions you make. Customize your equipment, ships, and special abilities to become legendary.
