The Inheritance of the First Sleeve Sword

The sleeve sword is the assassin's most common weapon. "Under the Sleeve Sword, All are Equal".

The Origin of the Sleeve Sword

The sleeve sword in the game was designed and manufactured by the Persian assassin Darius, whose sleeve sword was worn on the outside of his right arm and popped out when he clenched his fist. He assassinated King Xerxes I with his sleeved sword.
